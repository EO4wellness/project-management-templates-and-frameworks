# Professional Development

## Overview
This section provides resources that support **professional growth**, **career development**, and **continuing education** for Project Managers at all levels. While the main folders focus on PMBOK‑aligned project execution, this area is designed to help you strengthen the skills, habits, and learning pathways that sustain a long-term PM career.

## Purpose
Project Management is a profession that evolves continuously. Certifications such as **PIP CAPM** and **PIP PMP** require ongoing education, and PM roles increasingly demand competency across tools, communication, technical literacy, and domain-specific knowledge.

This folder contains curated guidance to help anyone:

- pivot into Project Management  
- build foundational PM skills  
- identify supplemental learning paths  
- maintain your certification while growing your career  
- develop professionally beyond the core PMBOK phases  

## What You’ll Find Here
This section includes mentoring-oriented, self-development resources such as:

- **PM Pivot Toolkit** – A concise roadmap for career changers entering PM especially currated for new Project Managers 
- **Continuing Education Guide** – Ways to maintain PIP certifications and earn PDUs  
- **Curated Course Lists** – High‑ROI learning options across PM software, technical literacy, operations, communication, and domain-specific PM  
- **30/60/90 PM Ramp-Up Roadmap** – Guidance for new PMs entering their first role  
- **PM Skills Map** – An overview of skills expected at junior, mid, and senior PM levels  
- **Mentoring Notes** – Practical advice and insights for developing as a PM  

## Who This Section Is For
- Those new to Project Management and/or career changes to PM 
- Junior PMs and PMO Analysts  
- Professionals preparing for CAPM/PMP  
- PMs seeking continuing education  
- Anyone building a long-term PM career  

## How to Use This Section
Browse the documents based on your current goals:

- If you’re **pivoting into PM**, start with the **New PM Toolkit**.  
- If you’re **new in a PM role**, use the **30/60/90 Roadmap**.  
- If you’re **maintaining certification**, review the **Continuing Education Guide**.  
- If you’re **building skills**, explore the curated course lists.  

This section is intentionally flexible and will grow over time as new mentoring and professional development resources are added.
