# Stakeholder Register Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure the Stakeholder Register is complete, accurate, and ready for use during project initiation and early planning.

---

## 1. Stakeholder Identification
- [ ] All individuals, groups, and organizations impacted by the project are identified  
- [ ] Internal and external stakeholders are included  
- [ ] Sponsor and key decision-makers are clearly listed  
- [ ] Subject matter experts (SMEs) are identified  
- [ ] Operational, compliance, and support teams are considered  
- [ ] End users or beneficiaries are included  
- [ ] No stakeholder groups are missing (use Thinking Guide prompts if unsure)

---

## 2. Stakeholder Details
- [ ] Roles/titles are accurate  
- [ ] Departments or groups are listed  
- [ ] Contact information is complete  
- [ ] Influence level is assessed (high/medium/low)  
- [ ] Interest level is assessed (high/medium/low)  
- [ ] Support level is estimated (supportive/neutral/resistant)  
- [ ] Notes capture relevant context or concerns  

---

## 3. Stakeholder Analysis
- [ ] Key stakeholders are clearly identified  
- [ ] Concerns, expectations, and motivations are documented  
- [ ] Potential conflicts or alignment issues are noted  
- [ ] Stakeholders with high influence or high interest are prioritized  
- [ ] Impact of each stakeholder on project success is assessed  

---

## 4. Engagement Strategies
- [ ] Engagement strategies are defined for each key stakeholder  
- [ ] Strategies align with influence and interest levels  
- [ ] Communication frequency is appropriate  
- [ ] Preferred communication channels are documented  
- [ ] Owners are assigned for each engagement activity  

---

## 5. Communication Requirements
- [ ] Stakeholder-specific communication needs are listed  
- [ ] Level of detail required is documented  
- [ ] Cadence is defined (weekly, monthly, milestone-based, etc.)  
- [ ] Communication methods are appropriate (email, meeting, dashboard, etc.)  
- [ ] Any required approvals or sign-offs are noted  

---

## 6. Risks & Issues Related to Stakeholders
- [ ] Stakeholder-related risks are identified  
- [ ] Impact and likelihood are assessed  
- [ ] Mitigation strategies are documented  
- [ ] Resistance or low engagement is addressed  
- [ ] Dependencies on stakeholders are noted  

---

## 7. Completeness & Quality Check
- [ ] Register is internally consistent  
- [ ] All fields are filled in or intentionally marked N/A  
- [ ] Information is current and validated  
- [ ] Document is clear, concise, and readable  
- [ ] Version history is updated  

# Final Review
- [ ] All key stakeholders are accounted for  
- [ ] Influence and interest ratings are consistent across the register  
- [ ] Engagement strategies align with stakeholder needs and project goals  
- [ ] Communication requirements are realistic and actionable  
- [ ] Stakeholder-related risks are documented and mitigation plans are clear  
- [ ] Register is ready for sponsor or PMO review  
- [ ] Version history is updated and accurate
