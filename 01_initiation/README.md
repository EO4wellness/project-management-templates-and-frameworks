# Initiation Templates and Tips 

This folder contains tools for initating any project with clarity and alignment. These templates support early communication, stakeholder alignment, and cross‑team understanding—bridging gaps before they appear.

Where appropriate, I've included **Templates**, which are fill-in-the-blanks documents, **Checklists** which provide a quick, but time-saving, answer to skim over and answer the "Did I forget anything?" question, and **Thought Guides** which offer deeper questions to help with reason through the initiation work.  These can be adapted for use in any project management or framework and are designed to be lightweight, practical and easy to integrate into real-project environments.  As an example, the frameworks and tools I've used them in include: Microsoft 365, Open Office, Google Workspace, ClickUp, Jira, etc).

## AI Tools in Project Management (Benefits, Risks, and Best Practices)
AI tools can be extremely helpful during project initiation, planning, and closing—especially for brainstorming, generating alternatives, or getting unstuck. However, responsible and safe use is essential.

### AI Tool Benefits:
- Faster brainstorming and idea generation
- Clearer thinking when starting a new project
- Drafting support for charters, communications, and documentation
- Help to identify risks, assumptions, and blind spots
- Troubleshooting tech tools
- Suggesting methods to clear roadblocks

### AI Tool Risks:
- Not all AI tools are NDA‑safe.
- Use of AI can fall outside organizational alignment or policy. 
- Sensitive or confidential information must never be entered. 
- AI outputs may be inaccurate or biased
- Over‑reliance can weaken critical thinking

###  Best Practices:
- Use AI only with NDA‑safe platforms
- Verify PM and Teams know project's safe use policy
- Keep prompts high‑level and non‑sensitive
- Validate all outputs with your own expertise
- Treat AI as a brainstorming partner, not a decision-maker
- Maintain human ownership of all project decisions
- As PM include agentic and other AI tools in honest cost/risk benefit analysis.
