## 11. Out‑of‑Scope Request Communication Pathway
This section defines the **operational workflow** for handling out‑of‑scope requests.

### 11.1 How Requests Are Submitted
- Requests must be submitted through:  
  - Email, form, PM tool, or designated channel  
- Requests must include:  
  - Description of the requested change  
  - Reason for the request  
  - Impact or urgency (if known)  

### 11.2 Logging & Tracking
- PM logs the request in the change log  
- PM assigns an initial category (scope, schedule, cost, quality)  
- PM acknowledges receipt to the requester  

### 11.3 Evaluation Process
- PM reviews the request with:  
  - Sponsor  
  - Relevant SMEs  
  - Impacted teams  
- Evaluation includes:  
  - Scope impact  
  - Timeline impact  
  - Budget/resource impact  
  - Risk impact  

### 11.4 Approval Path
- PM presents findings to the sponsor  
- Sponsor approves, rejects, or defers the request  
- High‑impact changes may require steering committee review  

### 11.5 Communication Back to Requester
- PM communicates the decision  
- PM updates the change log  
- If approved, PM updates:  
  - Scope  
  - Schedule  
  - Budget  
  - Communication Plan (if needed)
