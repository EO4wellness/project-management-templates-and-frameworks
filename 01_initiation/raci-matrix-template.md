# 2. Definitions
Use these definitions consistently across the matrix.

- **R – Responsible:**  
  The person(s) who performs the work or completes the task.

- **A – Accountable:**  
  The single owner of the task or deliverable. Approves work and ensures completion. **One** per task. 

- **C – Consulted:**  
  Provides input, expertise, or feedback before work is completed.

- **I – Informed:**  
  Needs visibility into progress or decisions but is not directly involved.
