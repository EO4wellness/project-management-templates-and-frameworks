# Project Charter Checklist

This checklist can be used to ensure the Project Charter is complete, aligned, and ready for sponsor approval.  Feel free to modify or update according to your specific needs and use case. 

---

## 1. Project Overview
- [ ] Project name is clear and descriptive  
- [ ] Project summary explains the problem/opportunity  
- [ ] Sponsor and PM are identified  
- [ ] Date and version are documented  

---

## 2. Purpose & Business Case
- [ ] Purpose clearly states *why* the project exists  
- [ ] Business case explains expected value or outcomes  
- [ ] Problem/opportunity is validated (not assumed)  
- [ ] Alignment with organizational goals is stated  

---

## 3. Objectives & Success Criteria
- [ ] Objectives are specific, measurable, and achievable  
- [ ] Success criteria accurately define what completion looks like  
- [ ] Metrics or indicators are included  
- [ ] Sponsor expectations are captured  

---

## 4. Scope Definition
- [ ] In-scope items are clearly listed  
- [ ] Out-of-scope items are explicitly stated  
- [ ] Boundaries prevent scope creep  
- [ ] Assumptions are documented  

---

## 5. Deliverables
- [ ] All major deliverables are listed  
- [ ] Deliverables are described clearly  
- [ ] Acceptance criteria (if known) are included  

---

## 6. Assumptions & Constraints
- [ ] Key assumptions are documented  
- [ ] Constraints (budget, time, resources, compliance) are listed  
- [ ] Dependencies are identified  

---

## 7. Risks & Issues (High-Level)
- [ ] Early risks are identified  
- [ ] Impact and likelihood are noted  
- [ ] Mitigation or response ideas are included  
- [ ] Known issues are documented  

---

## 8. Stakeholders (High-Level)
- [ ] Key stakeholders are listed  
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities are clear  
- [ ] Influence/interest levels are noted  
- [ ] Sponsor is clearly identified  

---

## 9. Milestones (High-Level)
- [ ] Major milestones are listed  
- [ ] Target dates are included  
- [ ] Dependencies or sequencing is noted  

---

## 10. Budget Summary (Optional)
- [ ] High-level budget estimate is included  
- [ ] Funding source is identified  
- [ ] Financial constraints are documented  

---

## 11. Approval
- [ ] Sponsor has reviewed the charter  
- [ ] Sponsor approval is documented  
- [ ] Date of approval is recorded  

---

# Final Review
- [ ] Charter is internally consistent  
- [ ] Language is clear and concise  
- [ ] Document is ready for distribution  
- [ ] Version history is updated
