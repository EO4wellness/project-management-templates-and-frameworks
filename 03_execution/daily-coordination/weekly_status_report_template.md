# Weekly Status Report  
Weekly status reports provide a structured, predictable way to communicate project progress, risks, changes, and upcoming priorities. They help stakeholders stay aligned, reduce surprises, and ensure the project remains on track against scope, schedule, and budget.

This toolkit includes a **Template**, a **Checklist**, a **Thinking Guide**, and a **Role‑Based Example** to demonstrate how weekly status reporting supports transparency and effective decision‑making.
