## Weekly Status Checklist

- Summarize progress clearly and concisely  
- Use role‑based ownership for clarity  
- Highlight blockers early  
- Update risks and issues with current status  
- Confirm dependencies are accurate  
- Identify decisions needed from leadership  
- Note any changes to scope, budget, or timeline  
- Keep the tone neutral, factual, and blameless  
- Include visuals (Gantt, roadmap, burndown) when helpful  
- Optionally use AI tools to generate summaries or detect patterns
