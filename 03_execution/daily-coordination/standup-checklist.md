# Standup Checklist
A quick reference to ensure your standup stays focused and useful.

- Keep the meeting under 15 minutes

- Focus on noting progress, extablishing priorities, and identifying blockers — not problem‑solving

- Capture blockers clearly and assign owners

- Identify any new risks or changes

- Confirm dependencies are on track

- Note items requiring escalation

- Update the Issue Log and Risk Register after the meeting

- Share a summary with stakeholders when needed

- Use AI tools to generate summaries or detect patterns in blockers
