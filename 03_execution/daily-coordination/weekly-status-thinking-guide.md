## Weekly Status Thinking Guide

**Accomplishments**  
- What moved the project forward?  
- Did we hit any milestones?  
- Did any deliverables reach Definition of Done?  

**Priorities**  
- What must be completed next week to stay on schedule?  
- Are priorities shifting due to risks or changes?  

**Risks & Issues**  
- What new risks emerged?  
- Did any risks escalate in severity?  
- Are issues trending or repeating?  
- Does anything require escalation?  

**Dependencies**  
- Are upstream teams on track?  
- Are downstream teams waiting on us?  
- Did any external vendors slip?  

**Changes**  
- Did scope, budget, or timeline shift?  
- Do we need a Change Request?  

**Decisions Needed**  
- What decisions are blocking progress?  
- Who needs to make them?  
- When are they required?
