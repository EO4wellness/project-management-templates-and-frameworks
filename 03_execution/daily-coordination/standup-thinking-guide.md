# Standup Thinking Guide
Questions to help teams reason through updates more effectively. Focus on what matters most. 

## Yesterday’s Progress

- What moved the project forward?

- Did anything take longer than expected? If so, why?

- Did any new risks appear?

## Today’s Priorities

- What is the single most important thing to complete today?

- Are priorities aligned with the project plan?

- Do we need to adjust the schedule?

## Blockers / Risks

- What is preventing progress?

- Does this impact a milestone, dependency, or deliverable?

- Does this require escalation?

- Can AI tools help identify similar past issues or suggest solutions?

## Dependencies

- Are upstream tasks on track?

- Are downstream teams waiting on us?

- Has anything changed in capacity or availability?

## Announcements / Changes

- Did scope, budget, or timeline shift?

- Are there new decisions from leadership?

- Do we need to update the Change Request Log?
