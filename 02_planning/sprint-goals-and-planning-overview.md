# Sprint Goals and Agile Planning Overview

Sprint goals anchor each iteration to a clear outcome. They help teams prioritize, reduce scope thrash, and maintain alignment with business value. 
This document also includes an overview diagram showing how Agile Planning fits into the broader Planning phase.

## Example Sprint Goals

### Example 1 — SaaS Feature
Deliver the first functional version of the new onboarding flow to improve user activation.

### Example 2 — Operations
Reduce ticket backlog by 20% and implement new triage rules.

### Example 3 — Healthcare
Complete workflow mapping for intake redesign and validate with clinical stakeholders.

## How Sprint Goals Fit Into Planning

Sprint goals connect the high‑level planning artifacts (Kickoff, WBS, budgeting) to the iterative execution cycle. They ensure each sprint contributes meaningfully to the project’s overall direction and help teams make tradeoffs when scope changes.

Sprint goals also reinforce:
- Clear prioritization  
- Predictable delivery  
- Alignment with stakeholders  
- Focus on value rather than volume
