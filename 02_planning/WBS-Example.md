## WBS Hierarchy (Hybrid Format)

### **1.0 Annual Company Appreciation Event**

---

### **1.1 Secure Venue**
- **1.1.1 Research venue options**  
  - 1.1.1.1 Identify potential venues  
  - 1.1.1.2 Gather pricing and availability  
- **1.1.2 Conduct venue tours**  
  - 1.1.2.1 Schedule tours  
  - 1.1.2.2 Evaluate layout and capacity  
- **1.1.3 Finalize venue selection**  
  - 1.1.3.1 Negotiate contract  
  - 1.1.3.2 Submit down payment  

---

### **1.2 Finalize Guest Logistics**
- **1.2.1 Create guest list**  
  - 1.2.1.1 Collect names from department leads  
  - 1.2.1.2 Verify contact information  
- **1.2.2 Send invitations**  
  - 1.2.2.1 Draft invitation content  
  - 1.2.2.2 Send via email platform  
- **1.2.3 Track RSVPs**  
  - 1.2.3.1 Monitor responses  
  - 1.2.3.2 Follow up with non-responders  

---

### **1.3 Establish Event Agenda**
- **1.3.1 Draft agenda outline**  
  - 1.3.1.1 Identify keynote speakers  
  - 1.3.1.2 Determine session topics  
- **1.3.2 Finalize agenda**  
  - 1.3.2.1 Confirm speaker availability  
  - 1.3.2.2 Publish final agenda  

---

### **1.4 Coordinate Catering**
- **1.4.1 Select catering vendor**  
  - 1.4.1.1 Research vendors  
  - 1.4.1.2 Request quotes  
- **1.4.2 Finalize menu**  
  - 1.4.2.1 Choose meal options  
  - 1.4.2.2 Confirm dietary accommodations  
- **1.4.3 Arrange delivery logistics**  
  - 1.4.3.1 Confirm delivery time  
  - 1.4.3.2 Assign setup responsibilities  

---

### **1.5 Prepare Event Materials**
- **1.5.1 Create signage and print materials**  
  - 1.5.1.1 Design signage  
  - 1.5.1.2 Print materials  
- **1.5.2 Assemble attendee packets**  
  - 1.5.2.1 Prepare name badges  
  - 1.5.2.2 Include agenda and handouts  

---

### **1.6 Execute Event Day Operations**
- **1.6.1 Set up venue**  
  - 1.6.1.1 Arrange seating  
  - 1.6.1.2 Set up registration table  
- **1.6.2 Manage event flow**  
  - 1.6.2.1 Coordinate speakers  
  - 1.6.2.2 Monitor schedule adherence  
- **1.6.3 Close out event**  
  - 1.6.3.1 Clean up venue  
  - 1.6.3.2 Conduct post-event debrief  

---

## Work Package Details (Examples)

### **Work Package: 1.1.2.1 Schedule tours**
- **Description:** Contact selected venues and schedule in-person or virtual tours.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Minimum of three venue tours scheduled; confirmations received.  
- **Dependencies:** Completion of 1.1.1.1 Identify potential venues.  
- **Estimated Duration:** 2 days  
- **Assigned To:** PM Coordinator  
- **Assumptions:** Venue staff availability.  
- **Constraints:** Tours must occur within the next two weeks.  

---

### **Work Package: 1.2.2.2 Send via email platform**
- **Description:** Send invitations using the company’s email marketing tool.  
- **Acceptance Criteria:** Invitations delivered to 100% of guest list.  
- **Dependencies:** Completion of 1.2.1.2 Verify contact information.  
- **Estimated Duration:** 1 day  
- **Assigned To:** Communications Lead  
- **Assumptions:** Email platform functioning normally.  
- **Constraints:** Must be sent at least 30 days before event.
