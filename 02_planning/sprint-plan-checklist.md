# Sprint Planning Checklist

## Pre‑Planning
- Product backlog is prioritized
- Top items have clear acceptance criteria
- Dependencies identified
- Team capacity validated (holidays, PTO, other work)
- Velocity from previous sprints reviewed

## During Planning
- Sprint goal defined and agreed upon
- Highest‑value items selected for the sprint
- Each item sized (points or hours)
- Team confirms feasibility based on capacity
- Risks and blockers discussed
- Definition of Done reviewed

## Post‑Planning
- Sprint backlog finalized
- Work assigned or pulled by team members
- Any required clarifications documented
- Board updated (Kanban or Scrum board)
