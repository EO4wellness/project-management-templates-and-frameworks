# Kanban Thinking Guide

- What is the minimum number of columns needed for clarity?
- Where does work typically get stuck? Should that be its own column?
- Do we need WIP limits to prevent overload?
- How will we ensure items are truly “Ready” before work begins?
- What does “Done” mean for this project?
- How will we visualize blocked work?
- How often will we groom the backlog?
